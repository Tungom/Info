Research Area: Artificial Intelligence for Optimization and Decision Making

Chia E. Tungom is an Algorithm Engineer at Vanke (OneWo Space-Tech Service Co.) where he researches and develops intelligent optimization algorithms for smart city and urban service operations. Prior to joining Vanke, he worked as an Algorithm Eningeer for Thousands Cities Strategic Algorithms Cloud Technologies (TCSA) at the National Data Brain Research Center from Sept. 2020 - Sept. 2021 where his team was responsible for the research, design and development of micro and macro economic demand and supply prediction frameworks for national level data informed policy decision making. He was a STEM instructor for Engeeker programming Academy where he designed and taught Python, Microbit and Scratch.

He is currently a Ph.D. researcher in Manganement Science and Economic Decision with the School of Management, Shenzhen University where his focus is on Swarm and Evolutionary Computing advised by Prof Ben Niu and Dr. Wang Hong. He obtained a Masters in Computer Science and Engineering from Xidian University in 2019 with his thesis on Automated Feature Engineering for Tabular Data advised by Dr Hui Li. He started his university education in the University of Buea in 2011 and obtained a Bachelor of Mechanical Enigneering from Ningbo University in 2016.

His work and research interest lies in the intersection of Computational Intelligence and Operations Research particularly in the development and application of Artificial Intelligence methods for Optimization and Decision Making.
